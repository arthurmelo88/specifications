openEHR Terminology

Introduction

This page provides access to the XML computable expression of the openEHR terminology. The openEHR terminology is an adjunct to the openEHR reference and archetype models, and provides code sets and vocabularies needed by the models. It is not a "real-world" ontology of any kind - i.e. it does not contain representation of facts in areas such as diseases or biochemistry - this is the job of much larger terminologies such as ICDx, ICPC, SNOMED-CT and so on.
